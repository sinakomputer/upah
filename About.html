Company Overview

Upah has been established as a platform connecting services to customers. In the advent of the internet, providers may generate business by matching their offering to customer looking for required services by the rates they can afford. Our platform aggregating score helps customer to determined the best provider suited to their budget and duration of works. Every provider and customer will go through a screening process to further provide credibility and reliability hence trust across all parties on getting the platform process to work across different region within Malaysia. Upah Sdn Bhd is trading on Malaysian Stock Exchange under the ticker UPH.
